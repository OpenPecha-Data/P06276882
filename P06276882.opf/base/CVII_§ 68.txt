[68] Then, having extolled the Elder Ānanda, the caravan leader, Divākara, arose, went up to
the lad Suvarṇavarṇa,together with his wife, embraced him tightly, and repeatedly covered
him with kisses.Greatly delighted and his eyes filled with tears of joy, looking steadily
at the lad Suvarṇavarṇa, he said: -

"By good fortune, I again see your body obtained after hundreds of wishes, and which surpasses
the glory and beauty of the splendour of the orb of the full moon in autumn. (1)On account
of the Elder Ānanda I see, again, your face with its eyes that are somewhat red at the
corners, elongated, dark and shining." (2)
